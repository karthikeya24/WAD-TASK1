DM stands for "direct message" and is a way to communicate privately with other users on Instagram. You can use DMs to interact with your community and answer their questions.

Here are some steps to send a DM on Instagram:

  • Open Instagram and log in to your account

  • Tap the paper airplane icon in the top right corner

  • Select your recipient(s) using the plus sign

  • Type your message, select a photo, or take a photo, and hit send

  • You can find your Instagram direct messages by tapping the "Home" icon and then the paper-plane icon in the top right corner.

  • Only you and the recipient can see the content of a direct message.
