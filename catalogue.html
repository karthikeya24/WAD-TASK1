A product catalog on Instagram is a collection of information about products that are sold on Instagram and Facebook. The quality of the catalog data is important for creating a positive shopping experience.

To turn on shopping features on Instagram, you can:

  1. Go to your business's Instagram profile
  2. Tap the menu icon
  3. Select Settings
  4. Tap Business and then Shopping
  5. Select the product catalog to connect to your account
  6. Select Done

To add products to the shop, you can:

  1. Go to the Instagram app
  2. Go to your profile and then View Shop
  3. Tap Menu and then Manage Shop
  4. Tap Products
  5. Tap Add products
  6. Select the products to add
  7. Tap Done
